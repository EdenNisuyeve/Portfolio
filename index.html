[01]

CAMPUS
PLUS

An app for HIT students with smart tools and AI support for easier campus life.

Mobile App UX Research UX/UI Design
view prototype

Starting at a new place can be exciting, but also overwhelming.
Many students struggle to find information, navigate different systems, and understand everyday processes.

As part of a course during my degree, We designed a solution that brings everything into one clear experience, using AI to make information easier to access and navigate.

The Challenge

The main challenge was to take a messy and unclear experience and turn it into something simple and easy to use.

Students get information from many different places, which makes things confusing and hard to follow. The challenge was to bring everything into one place, organize it in a clear way, and help students quickly find what they need without getting lost or frustrated.

At the same time, it was important to create something that students would actually use on a daily basis, and not just another system that exists but gets ignored.

The Solution

Designed a mobile app that brings campus information, student services, and daily academic tools into one simple and easy-to-use experience.

The app also includes an AI assistant that helps students quickly find answers, understand processes, and navigate campus life with less confusion.

The Process
1

Interviewed and collected feedback from over 10 HIT students from different years and departments

2

Learned what students struggle with and what information they need most

3

Created 5 student personas and planned simple user journeys

4

Designed wireframes and app screens with clear and easy navigation

5

Created a clean design based on HIT’s style and accessibility

6

Added and tested an AI chatbot to help students get quick answers and support

User Research & Personas

We interviewed over 10 HIT students from different years and study programs to better understand their needs and daily challenges. Based on the insights, we created 5 personas that represent different types of students and their goals.

Our Main Feature

What is HITbot?

A smart assistant that helps students quickly find information, contacts, and support based on their personal profile.

How it works

Students can ask HITbot any question or choose from common topics for quick help. Based on their profile, HITbot provides more relevant answers and guidance.

What HITbot can do

  • Answer common student questions
  • Connect students to the right office or contact
  • Share maps, links, and helpful information
  • Provide quick tech support guidance

Live Support

If needed, students can start a live video call with a real representative for fast and personal help.

[03]

Kit For
Managers

An interactive onboarding guide that helps managers support new employees in a clear and structured way.

Interactive Guide UX Research UX/UI Design
View full project

This project was developed as part of my final Bachelor's degree project, in collaboration with Playtika.

The project focuses on designing an interactive guide for managers, helping them support new employees throughout the onboarding process and ensuring no important steps are missed.

Instead of relying on scattered emails and documents, we created an interactive solution that provides clear, step-by-step guidance.

The goal was to improve the onboarding experience by making the process more structured, consistent, and easy to manage.

You can also view the project on the HIT website - right here!

The Challenge

Managers were responsible for a 45-day onboarding process but mainly relied on scattered emails and documents.

This created inconsistent onboarding experiences, unclear expectations, and made the process difficult to manage. Another challenge was designing a solution that felt engaging while remaining professional and suitable for managers.

The Solution

We designed an interactive onboarding guide that supports managers throughout the entire onboarding journey.

The experience is divided into clear stages and combines a playful, engaging approach with a professional look and feel, matching Playtika's culture while providing practical tools and guidance at every step.

The Process
1

Collaborated with the client to understand the onboarding process and identify gaps

2

Conducted user research to understand managers' needs and challenges

3

Defined key pain points and opportunities

4

Built the onboarding flow and content structure

5

Created wireframes and explored design concepts

6

Refined the solution through feedback and usability testing

7

Designed the final experience with a focus on clarity, consistency, and ease of use

User Research & Personas

Target Audience

The target audience was Playtika managers responsible for onboarding new employees. Most were based in Israel, with others located across Playtika's global offices.

The audience included both first-time and experienced managers from different departments, backgrounds, and levels of seniority.

To better understand managers' needs and challenges, we spoke with 15 managers across the organization.
Based on the insights, we created 3 personas representing different types of managers.

Ronen Michaeli

40, Development Team Lead, 7 Years at Playtika, Israel
"As an experienced manager, I'm looking for tools that help me remember all the critical steps in the process."

Yogev Danai

30, QA New Team Lead, 2 Years at Playtika, Israel
"As a newer manager, I'm looking for tools that help me lead the onboarding process with confidence."

Yulia Novak

41, Data Analysis Team Lead, 4 Months at Playtika, Warsaw
"I want to create the best onboarding experience possible, helping new employees feel part of the team as quickly as possible."

Names and images are fictional to protect privacy.

Evaluation & Validation

To ensure the solution met both user and business needs, we combined 3 methods:

1

Observation

Observing managers interacting with the onboarding experience in real time

2

Questionnaire

Collecting feedback through usability questionnaires

3

Expert Review

Conducting an expert review with a Learning & Development specialist responsible for onboarding processes

Key Findings

Managers found the content clear, useful, and relevant.

The step-by-step structure helped reduce information overload.

Interactive elements increased engagement without feeling unprofessional.

The design and language matched Playtika's culture and onboarding process.

Improvements Made

Added clearer navigation cues.

Improved the visibility of interactive elements.

Refined parts of the content flow for a smoother experience.

This process helped validate the solution and ensure it supports managers throughout the onboarding journey.
